THUSO Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

THUSO is committed to protecting the privacy and dignity of every person using our platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use and protect information submitted through THUSO.

Information We Collect

  • Information you choose to provide in a report.
  • Your contact details (only if you choose to provide them).
  • The date and time of submission.

How We Use Your Information

  • To receive and review reports.
  • To generate a unique case reference number.
  • To communicate with you if you provide an email address.
  • To refer cases to appropriate support services where applicable.

Your Privacy

Providing your personal information is optional. Anonymous reporting is supported where possible. Only authorised THUSO administrators can access submitted reports.

Data Security

THUSO stores submitted reports securely and limits access to authorised personnel. We take reasonable measures to protect information from unauthorised access.

Emergency Disclaimer

THUSO does not replace emergency services. If you are in immediate danger, please contact:

  • SAPS Emergency: 10111
  • GBV Command Centre: 0800 428 428
